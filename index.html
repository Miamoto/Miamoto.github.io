About Me

I am a student from Instituto Superior Técnico (IST), Lisbon, finishing my MSc in artificial intelligence for automatic speech recognition (ASR). Currently, I am also doing research in deep learning in the context of automatic speech recognition. I am particularly interested in studying unsupervised learning (without labels) and representation learning to advance artificial intelligence, and hopefully, understand the human brain. In my free time, I enjoy doing gesture drawing and playing the electric guitar. I received my BS from IST university, Lisbon.
