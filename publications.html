About Me

I am a student from Instituto Superior Técnico (IST), Lisbon, doing a PhD in artificial intelligence for low-resource automatic speech recognition (ASR). I am particularly interested in studying deep unsupervised learning and continual learning to advance artificial intelligence. In my free time I love to do draw and read. I received my MSc from IST university, Lisbon.
